Information Management and Information System
Information Management and Information System is an undergraduate program which is an interdisciplinary subject, dealing with management science, computer science and system science, with information system and information resource as its two research areas. The program, established by Donlinks School of Economics and Management, teaches fundamental theories and professional knowledge related to information system development and cultivates comprehensive professionals capable of information system development and management.
It’s a 4-year program for undergraduate students. A bachelor's degree in management is bestowed if the student has met the requirements of all kinds of evaluation.

Educational Objectives
The program aims to cultivate high-level professionals with ability and knowledge in areas including management, economics, law and computer science and technology, who are competent to conduct Information Management and Information System development or to be engaged in teaching & research of concerning areas.
