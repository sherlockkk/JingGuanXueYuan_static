Introduction to SEM of Jiangxi University of Science and Technology

Ⅰ. Goals for Professional Training
The School of Economics and Management (SEM) of Jiangxi University of Science and Technology (formerly named the Management Engineering Department of Jiangxi Institute of Metallurgy) was organized in 1980 and formally established in 1983, which is one of the first to set up the economics and management specialty among China's science and technology colleges and universities. After 30 years of unremitting efforts, the school has developed into a teaching and research school with a long history, excellent subject advantages, distinct characteristics, and abundant foreign exchanges.
The school has seven undergraduate specialties of Engineering Cost, Engineering Management, Accounting, Finance, E-commerce, Business Administration, and Marketing, wherein Engineering Management is a characteristic specialty of Jiangxi Province; Accounting is a branded specialty of Jiangxi Province. There are three master stations of first-class disciplines which are respectively the Business Administration, Management Science and Engineering (Management Science, Engineering), Applied Economics; five master stations of second-class disciplines of the Business Management, Technical and Economic Management, Accounting, Industrial Economics, and Finance; three Master’s degree stations of Business Administration (MBA), Master of Engineering Management (MEM), and Master of Professional Accounting (MPAcc); Master of Engineering training program of architectural and civil engineering (engineering management direction), with more than 2,000 undergraduates and about 500 postgraduates.
There are five departments of Business Administration, Engineering Management, Accounting, Finance, E-commerce, with about 90 staff, including 15 professors. The school has brought in a high level teaching staff of orderliness, reasonable structure, full juice, including well-known scholars and professors who have won in the New Century Excellent Talents Support Program of the Ministry of Education, the Trans-Century Academic Leaders of Ministry of Finance, Baosteel Education Award, the provincial “Hundred, Thousand and Ten Thousand Talent Project”, or Gan Po Talent 555 Project. The school has also hired a group of famous scholars and entrepreneurs as part-time professors or professional social master supervisors.
The school is aimed at serving the regional economic construction and mining industry development. The People's Government of Jiangxi Province, the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology of the People’s Republic of China, and Ministry of Education are co-building the Jiangxi University of Science and Technology. Under this background, the school is actively seeking the supports from provincial and municipal government departments and related enterprises public institution. The school is building the Ganzhou Financial Research Institute, Ganzhou Industrial Development Institute, and Ganzhou Regional Economic and Social Development Research Center together with Ganzhou Municipal People’s Government. The school has been approved as Jiangxi Nonferrous Metal Industry Development Research Base, Jiangxi Mining Development Research Base, Regional Economic Cooperation Soft Science Research Base in Jiangxi, Guangdong, Fujian and Hunan Border, and other provincial research centers. There are Marketing Research Center, Rare Earth Industry Planning Institute, Engineering Economy and Management Research Institute, System Engineering Research Institute and other academic institutions. The school is trying to become a research center of regional economic revitalization and development, an academic exchange center and a think tank for the government.
The school is aimed at cultivating internationalized economic management personnel with global vision, and has built a good cooperation with the State University of New York, Coastal Georgia Community College, University of Plymouth, Canada University of Prince Edward Island, Thailand Prince of Songkla University and other universities on teaching, scientific research, talents cultivation and other aspects, and established the Business Administration "2+2" international class with University of Prince Edward Island. The school has carried out many student exchange projects with overseas universities. Foreign teachers are participated in college teaching activities all year round, which helped the school to get remarkable achievements in international teaching.
The college will be adhering to the school motto of "Inflate Your Ambition; Responsibility is the First" to cultivate comprehensive and innovative talents, deepen the connotation construction, service for the regional economic and social development and related industries, and strive to improving the ability and visibility.

Ⅱ. Requirements of Professional Training
Students mainly study the basic theory and knowledge of management, computer and economics international trade and so on, and would get trained in basic theory and techniques of e-commerce, finally having primary ability to analyze problems, solve problems and make decision. Graduates should obtain following knowledge, ability and quality: 1. Students master basic theory of economy, management, international trade and other aspects; 2. Master Web techniques and own fundamental knowledge and ability to work on e-commerce website development; 3. Master theories and methods of network marketing, logistics management and database, possess ability to actually operate and manage with network marketing, logistics management, international trade and others; 4 Have a good command of a foreign language; be skilled in reading e-commerce professional foreign language materials and possess certain ability in practical work and scientific research.

Ⅲ. Education System and Degrees Conferred
Education System 4 years, Bachelor of Management

Ⅳ. Major Disciplines
Management science and engineering, business administration, information management and system
