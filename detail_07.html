Profession Introduction of Marketing

Ⅰ. Goals for Professional Training
(1) Professional Profile: Marketing major was founded in September 2009, which combines existing actual conditions of the school and brings diverse advantages such as hardware facilities and faculty into full play, aiming to take the characteristic professional construction as the goal. Based on full market research and expert certification, try to accurately locate training objectives of marketing professionals, and establish a professional education philosophy that "is market demand-oriented, takes entity, practical training, actual combat as reform direction and cultivation of students’ comprehensive ability as the center, as well as the industry as the feature " , and confirm training ideas of professionals that " set up majors according to the market decide courses as needed". (2) Professional training goals: The professional train advanced professional personnel who own knowledge and ability of business administration, marketing management or human resources management and other aspects and can work on teaching of marketing or human resources management, scientific research in the enterprises and institutions as well as government departments.

Ⅱ. Requirements of Professional Training
Students mainly study the basic theory and knowledge of business administration, marketing management or human resource management, and are fundamentally trained with management methods and skills, and finally can have primary ability to analyze and solve the management problems. Graduates should obtain following knowledge, ability and quality: 1. Master the basic theory, knowledge and qualitative, quantitative analysis methods of management, economics, marketing management or human resource management; 2. Core competence of this major is marketing operation, planning and operation management, and human resource management; 3. Have strong ability in literal expression, interpersonal communication, information acquisition and analysis as well as the ability of solving the practical management problems; 4. Know related principles, policies and regulations and international management practices of domestic and foreign enterprises management well; 5. Understand the theoretical frontier and the development trend of the discipline; 6. Master basic method for the literature retrieval, data query and a certain degree of scientific research and practical work ability

Ⅲ. Education System and Degrees Conferred
Education System 4 years, Bachelor of Management

Ⅳ. Major Disciplines
Marketing, HRM, Economics and Management

Ⅴ. Major Courses
1. Discipline Fundamental Courses: economic mathematics; management; financial management; HRM; Western economics; CIS planning; e-commerce; statistics; public relations; advertising; logistics management; business English; commercial law. 2. Professional Core Courses: marketing; customer praxiology; marketing investigation and analysis; business negotiations; customer relationship management; marketing planning; corporate strategic management; cyber marketing. 3. Professional Specialty Courses: production and operation management, brand management; theories and techniques of marketing; retailing; business writing; speech and eloquence; accounting report analysis; franchise; ERP system; management operations research; securities investment; marketing management special; bidding and contract management; etc.
