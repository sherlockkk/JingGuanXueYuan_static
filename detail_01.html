Professional Introduction of E-commerce

Ⅰ. Goals for Professional Training
(1) Professional Profile: E-commerce undergraduate major was founded in 2002 and is the second batch nationwide, the first batch of e-commerce undergraduate major in Jiangxi Province. In 1999, the Institute of Economics and Management of the major was given the gift of the master's degree in management science and engineering (including e-commerce direction). In 2002, it got the gift of Master's degree in business management (including logistics management direction). In 2007, it obtained the master pilot in logistics management engineering. After more than 10 years’ development, e-commerce has formed its own professional characteristics, and primarily developed e-commerce system development and design, network marketing and data analysis, logistics management these three professional directions. This major pays attention to cultivate innovation and entrepreneurship ability of students, and actively encourage students to participate in national college student electronic (Internet) business competition, "Challenge Cup" national college students’ extracurricular scientific and technical competition and other competitions. (2) Professional Training Goals: Intermediate and advanced e-commerce professional personnel that own solid economic and management theory, also are familiar with the Internet and Web technology, master e-commerce principles and technology, e-commerce website development, network marketing, logistics management, international trade and other knowledge, and can work on e-commerce applications, development and management.

Ⅱ. Requirements of Professional Training
Students mainly study the basic theory and knowledge of management, computer and economics international trade and so on, and would get trained in basic theory and techniques of e-commerce, finally having primary ability to analyze problems, solve problems and make decision. Graduates should obtain following knowledge, ability and quality: 1. Students master basic theory of economy, management, international trade and other aspects; 2. Master Web techniques and own fundamental knowledge and ability to work on e-commerce website development; 3. Master theories and methods of network marketing, logistics management and database, possess ability to actually operate and manage with network marketing, logistics management, international trade and others; 4 Have a good command of a foreign language; be skilled in reading e-commerce professional foreign language materials and possess certain ability in practical work and scientific research.

Ⅲ. Education System and Degrees Conferred
Education System 4 years, Bachelor of Management

Ⅳ. Major Disciplines
Management science and engineering, business administration, information management and system

Ⅴ. Major Courses
1. Discipline Fundamental Courses: economic mathematics (Ⅰ), object-oriented programming (C#), western economics (D), management (B), economic mathematics (Ⅱ), introduction to e-commerce, introduction to database system, accountancy (B), website graphic design, e-commerce economics, management operational research, financial management (E), web programming. 2. Professional Core Courses: e-commerce logistics management, management information systems, computer networks and business security, market research and statistical analysis, network marketing, international trade and theoretical practice, e-commerce system analysis and design (A). 3. Professional Elective Courses: E-commerce entrepreneurial management, marketing (B), statistics, production and operation management, e-commerce law (B), customer relationship management (B), mobile e-commerce, search engine optimization, advanced technology of data analysis (B), e-payment and e-bank, internet finance, HRM (B), e-commerce project management, tourism e-commerce, supply chain management, international freight forwarding, customs practice, ERP special, e-commerce English, e-government, JAVA language programming.
