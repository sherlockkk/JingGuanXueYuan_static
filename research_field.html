Introduction to Research Field of SEM

Ⅰ. Methods and application of management science
This direction mainly takes the basic theory and method of management and major complexity management issues as study object, especially the quantification and mathematical modeling, and applies them on human resources development and management, production and operation and investment decision of non-ferrous metal mining and metallurgical enterprises. After several years of preparation, this direction tries to make progress on quantitative analysis of human resources development and management, enterprise production and operation simulation study, enterprise safety management study, enterprise equipment management study and others, to be in the leading position on the quantitative analysis of human resources development and management, and the research on large-scale comprehensive enterprises production and operation simulation system, and to gradually form an influence among the worldwide industry.

Ⅱ. Research on regional and industrial development strategy
Together with the opportunities of the rise of central China strategy and the advantages of Jiangxi nonferrous metal and steel industries, this direction uses modern research methods of Management Science and Economics to conduct quantification and modeling research on regional and industrial development issues, and has achieved innovative results on researching the development of nonferrous metal and steel industry, and development of regional economic. The direction has generally reached the domestic top level. In the aspects of nonferrous metal and steel industry strategy, circular economy, and regional industrial cluster network research and others, this direction has reached the leading position in domestic.

Ⅲ. Information technology and management
This direction has a distinctive research advantage on the Web data mining, information system security management, mine metallurgy information system and geographic information system. The theory and application research in these fields will be enhanced in future research. In theory, this direction emphasizes the research on intelligent computing, neural network, network security digital communication, genetic programming, gene expression programming, embedded evolution system, concurrent computation and so on. In application, this direction emphasizes the research on development of information technology and mining information system, data mining and data warehouse, knowledge management and organization study, comprehensive utilization research on GIS and mineral resources, and so on. Research on informatization of nonferrous metals and steel industry has reached the domestic top level.

Ⅳ. Project management
This direction relies on the characteristics of industry and investment project; based on the research on theory and method of original engineering cost management, the direction emphasizes the research on project investment, financing, organization and other aspects, and strengthens the evaluation theory and method research on the mining and metallurgical project, forming the research advantages and characteristics on large-scale complex mining and metallurgy project management. This direction has reached the leading position in domestic in general as well as in the engineering cost management.

Ⅴ. Human resources management
The research team, including Professor Ye Rensun as the leader, focus on the use of quantitative methods in the human resources management research, such as structural equation mode, panel data analysis, system dynamics, etc. They also have deep research on enterprise post evaluation, relationship between scientific & technological progress and employment and have made a series of innovative results. The key project of Ministry of Science and Technology -- Empirical Analysis on Scientific and Technological Progress on the Employment of Less Developed Areas -- has been awarded to “have reached the domestic leading level” and been published in Soft Science Reporting held by Ministry of Science and Technology in December 2006 for the provinces, municipalities and ministries’ reference. The Jiangxi Provincial major social and economic bidding project -- Research on the Rise of Jiangxi and Talent Issues has been rated as good report, which report has obtained the instructions from the governor assistant Xiong Shengwen. The Ministry of Railways major tender project -- Research on Post Evaluation of Rail Transport Enterprise – has been awarded to “have reached the domestic leading level” The research team has published over 20 articles on the Management World, Quantitative & Technological Economics and other well-known publications, and over four articles have been fully reprinted by the Chinese People's Congress newspaper Human Resources Development and Management and Labor Economy and Labor Relations. The research results have been awarded a first prize and two second prizes in Jiangxi Social Sciences Outstanding Achievements Award, and a second prize in National Personnel Scientific Research Achievement. This direction has strong potentiality, with three professors and 4 Dr.s in the research team. The direction now is undertaking several national and provincial scientific research projects such as the project from National Planning Office of Philosophy and Social -- Studies on the Talents in The Economic Development in Central and Western Regions, and the key soft science research project from Ministry of Railways-- Research on Railway Labor Management System Design.

Ⅴ. Major Courses
Xie Yingliang, Peng Pin et al, by use of the theory and method of technical economy, system engineering and financial engineering, have studied and applied the method and model of mining planning and investment and decision. They have also adopted the advanced option pricing theory to complete the major soft tender project from Jiangxi Provincial Department of Science and Technology -- Research on Jiangxi Non-ferrous Metal Industry Development Strategy, which results has been awarded the second prize in 11th Jiangxi Provincial Social Science Outstanding Achievement Awards 2006. Xie Yingliang, Peng Pin et al, by use of the marginal analysis, artificial neural network, genetic algorithm, etc., have studied the issues such as scheme selecting, grade index optimization, production scheduling optimization, etc. and have completed the article -- Economic Analysis on Marginal Grade Optimization of Strip Mine. The results have had a great impact in the industry and academia, and obtained the third prize in Jiangxi Provincial 13th Social Science Outstanding Achievement 2009. Based on the theory of sustainable development, Huang Xiaodong et al have studied nonferrous metals industry plan, which is based on the circular economy, by use of the theory and method of industrial organizations and industrial policy, etc. They have completed several provincial and ministerial projects such as the Jiangxi provincial social sciences planning project -- Analysis on Jiangxi Nonferrous Metals Industry Development Issues under Circular Economy Mode, Analysis on Ganzhou Tungsten Ore Mining and Processing Industry Policy and Tungsten Industry Development Strategy. The research results have been posted on System Engineering Theory and Practice and other international publications. Rao Yunzhang, Xu Shuitai et al have integrated the modern evaluation theory and method to conduct the research on mining area environmental assessment index system, safety management evaluation and implementation, and have completed the Natural Science Foundation of China project-- Environmental Quality Evaluation Index and Management Research on Mine Geotechnical Engineering System. Several articles have been published on China Mining Magazine and other journals and in international conferences. There is a dedicated research team in this research direction, including two professors and four Dr.s with high research reputation in domestic. This direction is now undertaking several provincial and ministerial research projects such as the soft science project from Ministry of Science and Technology -- Sustainable Development Research on China's Non-Ferrous Metal Industry, the state Ministry of Human Resources returned overseas students projects-- Large-scale Comprehensive Enterprises Production and Operation Simulation System, Jiangxi social science planning project-- Research on Eco-Industrial System Construction Planning of Jiangxi Tungsten Industry .

Ⅶ. Enterprise information management
On the mining enterprises, Professor Guo Nianqin et al have studied and developed the maintenance and management expert system for mine machinery and equipment, the mine resource allocation system, and the mine enterprise MIS, etc., which provide powerful technical means to improve the production and decision-making efficiency for mining enterprises. They also have been applied it in mining engineering system control and expert system mode construction and so on. This research direction has obtained a series of research results, and completed several provincial projects such as the "Expert System for Mine-Concentrator Equipment Maintenance Management", and has obtained the second prize in Jiangxi Provincial University Science and Technology Achievement Award 2006. Que Shipeng et al have been committed to the research of mathematical mode in enterprise management, providing the analysis and calculation basis on forecast, decision and optimization for computer assisted management. They also have published several articles about mining production and management decision mode on famous publications such as China Coal Society and Metal Mine.      Professor Li Jian, leader of e-commerce research team of our school, has been committed in research on development and application of private e-commerce, and has deeply analyzed consumer’s e-commerce adoption behavior and studied the e-commerce talent training and entrepreneurship mode. He used to guide students to achieve excellent results in the 1st National Undergraduate Electronic Commerce "Innovation, Creativity and Entrepreneurship" Challenge, National College Students' Contest on Network Commerce innovation application, and has completed several research projects such as the E-Commerce Development and Application on Private Enterprise. Xu Ling, Jia Yanglei et al have deeply studied on logistics management informatization, aimed to achieve the informatization throughout the logistics decision, business processes, and customer service, and to realize scientific logistics management. This research field mainly focuses on the information sharing management, the logistics information sharing platform management and the reverse logistics mode in the logistics management. Several high quality academic articles have been published, wherein 3 articles are included by EI and 2 articles are copied by the NPC. The teaching staff in this research direction is strong, including 2 professors and 1 Dr. with rich experience on teaching and research. Currently, they are undertaking several provincial and ministerial projects such as the Jiangxi Provincial Department Of Science and Technology project -- Technical Advisory Services and Training System Construction on Manufacturing Informatization Science and Technology Engineering, Jiangxi provincial major tender project – 3D CAD Enterprise Application Demonstration, Jiangxi provincial social science planning project -- Research on University E-commerce Personnel Training and Entrepreneurial Mode.

Ⅷ. Financial management and emerging accounting research
This direction has formed its own research characteristics on the aspects of the financial forecast, investment project evaluation and mining rights pricing and others. For example, Liu Jianbing et al have studied on the intangible assets valuation, investment project evaluation, and cost optimization and control by use of system dynamics, marginal analysis methods and other advanced tools. Over 10 of their published articles have been included by EI or ISTP. They used to provide financial optimization decision advice for Shaoguan Smelter Plant and other enterprises. Xie Linhai et al have applied the system dynamic theory and financial software to the financial forecast, and published a monograph -- System Dynamics Applications on Enterprise Financial Management. Liao Zuhong et al have studied the option pricing theory and method of mining rights. After doing the investigation and research on the capital market, they combined with the characteristics of mine production and management and put forward a set of highly operational mining rights pricing methods. Many of their articles have been published on China Mining and other publications. Bai Tiannan et al have studied the financial management mode of SMEs board listed companies at home and abroad. The results have achieved the third prize in Jiangxi provincial 13th Social Science Outstanding Achievement Awards. Under the trends of economic development globalization, sustainable development, administrative system reform, new requirements will be raised on accounting theory and practice. The research will focus on anti-dumping response accounting and environmental accounting and other emerging accounting.      Zhou Junqing et al, with the anti-dumping investigation enterprises as the research object, and have studied the accounting issues related to anti-dumping and complaints process. Chang Yuan et al have studied the enterprise environmental cost accounting based on the value chain theory, and achieved certain results. Ying Yihua et al have studied the reform of budget accounting and the construction of government accounting by use of public management theory and the classification of government revenue and expenditure, so as to determine the accounting basis of government accounting and the main body of government accounting, to improve the contents and form of budget statements, and to reduce accounting information distortion. In recent years, this direction has recruited and trained over 10 graduate students in financial management from the MBA station. In this direction, there are 1 professor, 5 associate professors, and 1 Dr. They are now undertaking several provincial and ministerial projects such as Cash Flow Forecast System Dynamics Simulation Model of Colleges and Universities, Dynamic Simulation System Research on Enterprise Financial Forecast and Financial Diagnosis, Early Warning System of China Anti-Dumping Industry to US Based on Strategic Management Accounting.

Ⅸ. Regional economic theory research
Teng Anqing et al, on the basis of previous studies, are building a new set of regional economic space and network theory-- that is the economic T space and its N network theory, with the philosophy as basics, mathematics (set theory, topology, fractal geometry) as the means, space, time and energy as the content, and economic network and the economy as the point of view. They have used mathematical means to abstract and solidify the relevant academic ideas about new theoretical framework, and initially formed their own theoretical framework. The direction has held 5 provincial and ministerial projects, and published over 20 articles, such as Development Zone Industrial Cluster Research Based on Economic ETPN Economic Network Theory（Jiangxi colleges and universities humanities）held by Teng Qingan, Red Triangle Economic Circle Economic Network Based on Economic Point Network Theory（Jiangxi colleges and universities humanities）held by Zhu Wenxing. The method and application of regional economic analysis is an important research direction in our regional economics. By use of the mathematical methods such as cellular automaton, set theory, topology, fractal geometry, etc., our teaching staff have combined the structural analysis of the point, line and surface of the network, and established a set of theoretical research outlines. They also use the system dynamics, input and output, cellular automaton, system simulation, fractal geometry and other analysis and evaluation methods to do long-term study on regional or industrial economy field, forming an obvious characteristic. Teng Qingan et al have studied the fractal evolution rule of economic network by use of fractal geometry; Huang Xiaodong et al have studied the circular economy of nonferrous metal industry by use of system dynamics method, and made some innovations in theory and research method, and achieved good results; Zhu Wenxing has analyzed the regional economic network system integration by use of the system; Huangxue Liang et al and have studied the coordinated development of the local economic sectors and the input and output efficiency , the research methods in which are innovative. On combining local industry characteristics, they have studied the local industrial clusters; Professor Teng Qingan has studied the industry cluster evolution based on cellular automata; Professor Huang Shunchun has studied the local industry cluster changes; Huang Xiaodong has studied the sustainable development in Dongjiang based on region system dynamics; Huang Xuoliang has studied the regional industry evaluation and sustainable development; Peng Feng and Zheng Minggui et al have studied the non-ferrous metal mine scale efficiency based on the scale economies analysis method. In this direction, there’s a young and strong academic team, including two professors and three Dr.s, who have rich experience in teaching and research. Innovation and application research has always been our mission. We are now involved in over 10 provincial and ministerial projects and over 10 local government projects. Over 70 articles have been published on Economic Geography, Industrial Technology & Economy and other well-known publications. With the globalization of economy, regional harmonious development issues have become increasingly prominent. This direction will have a stronger academic vitality and a brighter future.

Ⅹ. Mining and nonferrous metal industry economy
Academic staff in Industrial economics professional, relying on the background of non-ferrous smelting and mining engineering, have been committed to the non-ferrous industry economic research for a long time, and has achieved a high academic status and high-level research results, and formed the following features: (1) Mining development planning and mining structure adjustment. Rao Yunzhang and Huang Xiaodong, by use of the theory and method of industrial structure adjustment and system engineering, have studied and applied the methods and modes of mining planning and industrial policy. They have used the advanced system dynamics theory to promote the relevant theories and methods. (2) Non-ferrous industry development planning and industrial policy. Rao Yunzhang, Lin Ruhai et al, by use of the theory and method on industry organization and policy, have studied the nonferrous metals industry development plan and industry policy formulation, wherein the study results have been applied to a certain extent. (3) Structure and layout of non-ferrous mining and smelting industry. Lin Ruhai, Xu Shenghua et al, by use of the theory and method on industrial structure and regional industrial layout, have studied the theory and application of structure and layout of local special industry (mining). Research results have been adopted by local governments, and achieved huge social and economic benefits. In this direction, there’s a young and hard-working research team. In recent years, they have undertaken eight provincial or ministerial projects such as the soft science project from Jiangxi Provincial Department of Science and Technology -- Research on Jiangxi Non-Ferrous Metal Industry Development Strategy, wherein the research results have obtained a good social evaluation. In recent years, they have obtained four provincial and ministerial scientific and technological awards. Many projects have been applied in practice. They also have achieved valuable social and economic benefits. More than 70 articles have been published on the China Journal of Management Science, Technology Economics and other well-known publications.
