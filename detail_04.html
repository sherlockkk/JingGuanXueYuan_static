Profession Introduction of Business Administration (Sino-foreign Cooperative Education)

Ⅰ. Goals for Professional Training
(1) Professional Profile: International classes of Business Administration enroll students for the first time in 2013, based on the goal of cultivating extroverted, multi-skilled, application-oriented international professionals, which, by studying the basic theory, knowledge and methods of modern management, can master the basic theory of Business Administration and skillfully use management methods and skills, with strong ability of English using and strong ability to analyze the problem and deal with basic service of business management, as well as business management personnel with global perspectives. After graduation, students can take up occupations of management in diverse enterprises, public institutions and government departments or domestic and foreign multinational corporations, international commercial institutions and organizations. (2) Professional Training Goals: this major train students to master the basic principles of management and economics as well as the basic knowledge of modern business management, to understand business management environment, and to be familiar with the relevant policies, regulations and international business management practices and the rules of foreign and domestic business management, in order to cultivate advanced professional personnel of Business Administration who can work on teaching of marketing or human resources management, scientific research in the enterprises and institutions as well as government departments.

Ⅱ. Requirements of Professional Training
Students mainly study the basic theory and knowledge of business administration, marketing management or human resource management, and are fundamentally trained with management methods and skills, and finally can have primary ability to analyze and solve the management problems. Graduates should obtain following knowledge, ability and quality: 1. Master the basic theory, knowledge and qualitative, quantitative analysis methods of management, economics, marketing management or human resource management; 2. Have a solid foundation in mathematics, English and other natural sciences, as well as a fine basis of humanistic and social science and basic computer application ability; 3. Have proficient ability of using the English language; 4. Know related principles, policies and regulations and international management practices of domestic and foreign enterprises management well; 5. Have certain degree of literal expression, interpersonal communication, information acquisition and analysis as well as the ability of solving the practical management problems; 6. Master basic method for the literature retrieval, data query and a certain degree of scientific research and practical work ability.

Ⅲ. Education System and Degrees Conferred
Education System 4 years, Bachelor of Management
