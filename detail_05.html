Professional Introduction of Accountancy

Ⅰ. Goals for Professional Training
(1) Professional Profile: Accountancy began to recruit accounting and auditing junior college students in 1989, accounting undergraduate students in 1996 and it was rated as Brand Specialty in Jiangxi Province in 2004, in 2012 it recruits accounting master degree candidates; Now there are 14 teachers, 12 teachers who has associate professors and above titles (6 tutors of master accountants), 5 certified public accountants and 2 registered tax agents; There are multiple laboratories with accounting manual simulation, accounting informationization, ERP sand table simulation, and equipped with UFIDA, Kingdee, VPDN and other teaching software, emphasizing equal attention in theory and practice, strengthening English ability, computer application ability, accounting information application ability and practical ability training of students, focusing on cultivating comprehensive quality and professional skills students in order to meet the needs of actual work. (2) Professional Training Goals: This major trains practice-oriented advanced professional personnel with innovative thinking and practical ability, which own knowledge and ability of management, economy, law, accountancy and so on, master basic theory and professional skills in financial accounting, financial management, accounting information systems and auditing etc. and can work on accountancy, financial management and auditing in the enterprises and institutions as well as government departments.

Ⅱ. Requirements of Professional Training
Students mainly study the basic theory and knowledge of management, computer and economics international trade and so on, and would get trained in basic theory and techniques of e-commerce, finally having primary ability to analyze problems, solve problems and make decision. Graduates should obtain following knowledge, ability and quality: 1. Students master basic theory of economy, management, international trade and other aspects; 2. Master Web techniques and own fundamental knowledge and ability to work on e-commerce website development; 3. Master theories and methods of network marketing, logistics management and database, possess ability to actually operate and manage with network marketing, logistics management, international trade and others; 4 Have a good command of a foreign language; be skilled in reading e-commerce professional foreign language materials and possess certain ability in practical work and scientific research.

Ⅲ. Education System and Degrees Conferred
Education System 4 years, Bachelor of Management

Ⅳ. Major Disciplines
Business administration, economics and law

Ⅴ. Major Courses
1. Discipline Fundamental Courses: economic mathematics, management, microeconomics, macroeconomics, finance, statistics, economics law, basic accounting, financial management, accounting information system, corporate strategy and management accounting. 2. Professional Core Courses: intermediate financial accounting (Ⅰ), intermediate financial accounting (Ⅱ), advanced financial accounting, tax law and tax accounting, cost accounting, management accounting, auditing. 3. Professional Elective Courses: contemporary world economy and politics, marketing, SQL database application technology, financial regulations and professional ethics, management information systems, professional English, tax planning, logistics and supply chain management, comparative accounting, securities investment, Excel applications in accounting, HRM, international trade, personal finance, financial analysis, e-commerce, asset assessment, financial special, accounting special, international accounting standards, government and non-profit organization accounting, ERP systems, accounting system design.
