Professional Introduction of Engineering Cost

Ⅰ. Goals for Professional Training
(1) Professional Profile: This major began to recruit undergraduates facing the country since 1986, and is the first undergraduate major of the national engineering management (engineering cost management), which has cultivated to train nearly 3,000 undergraduates for the society. Pass rate of registered cost engineers always comes the first and enjoys a very high reputation in the field of engineering cost. In 1996, this major was identified as key construction discipline of Jiangxi Province and in 2002 was approved as a brand specialty in Jiangxi Province. In 1995, the major began to recruit postgraduates, and formed a multi-level pattern of unified enrollment of master students, engineering master students, masters of equivalent and undergraduates, which highlights the characteristics that based on engineering technology and featured engineering cost control in the whole life cycle. Numerous students recognized this major as a hot specialty which recruits students the most easily and have the best job opportunities. (2) Professional Training Goals: This major cultivates application-oriented and investigative personnel who possess fundamental knowledge of management, economy, civil engineering technology, master theories, methods and means in modern management science, and have strong practical application ability, social adaption ability and certain innovation ability, as well as can work on cost calculation and audit, engineering decision-making, project management of engineering cost in the field of engineering construction management.

Ⅱ. Requirements of Professional Training
Students mainly study the basic theory and methods of knowledge and economy of construction engineering technology, project management; and would get trained in project management especially engineering cost management; finally have primary ability to work on determination and control of engineering cost as well as engineering project management. Graduates should obtain following knowledge, ability and quality: 1. Familiar with and master the basic knowledge, theories and methods of civil engineering technology, and have the basic ability to analyze civil engineering problems; 2. Familiar with and master the basic knowledge of civil engineering measurement, with the ability to prepare and audit the project investment estimation, rough calculation, budgeting, and account settlement; 3. Familiar with and master the basic principles and methods of management, and have the fundamental ability to use the knowledge for project management; 4. Familiar with and grasp the basic principles and methods of economics, especially engineering economics, and have the basic ability to use this knowledge for project decision-making and analysis. 5. Familiar with the principles, policies and regulations of the project construction, understand the development trend of foreign and domestic project management; 6. Have the basic ability to use advanced technology (such as computer technology) for project management, as well as primary ability to make further learning and scientific research on the basis of original knowledge; 7. Familiar with and master the use of foreign languages, information retrieval and other means to inquire information, and possess fundamental ability in scientific research and practical work; 8. Have strong self-study ability, innovation consciousness and certain social competence.
