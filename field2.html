Introduction to Research Field of SEM

Ⅰ . Project management
This direction relies on the characteristics of industry and investment project; based on the research on theory and method of original engineering cost management, the direction emphasizes the research on project investment, financing, organization and other aspects, and strengthens the evaluation theory and method research on the mining and metallurgical project, forming the research advantages and characteristics on large-scale complex mining and metallurgy project management. This direction has reached the leading position in domestic in general as well as in the engineering cost management.

Ⅱ. Human resources management
The research team, including Professor Ye Rensun as the leader, focus on the use of quantitative methods in the human resources management research, such as structural equation mode, panel data analysis, system dynamics, etc. They also have deep research on enterprise post evaluation, relationship between scientific & technological progress and employment and have made a series of innovative results. The key project of Ministry of Science and Technology -- Empirical Analysis on Scientific and Technological Progress on the Employment of Less Developed Areas -- has been awarded to “have reached the domestic leading level” and been published in Soft Science Reporting held by Ministry of Science and Technology in December 2006 for the provinces, municipalities and ministries’ reference. The Jiangxi Provincial major social and economic bidding project -- Research on the Rise of Jiangxi and Talent Issues has been rated as good report, which report has obtained the instructions from the governor assistant Xiong Shengwen. The Ministry of Railways major tender project -- Research on Post Evaluation of Rail Transport Enterprise – has been awarded to “have reached the domestic leading level” The research team has published over 20 articles on the Management World, Quantitative & Technological Economics and other well-known publications, and over four articles have been fully reprinted by the Chinese People's Congress newspaper Human Resources Development and Management and Labor Economy and Labor Relations. The research results have been awarded a first prize and two second prizes in Jiangxi Social Sciences Outstanding Achievements Award, and a second prize in National Personnel Scientific Research Achievement. This direction has strong potentiality, with three professors and 4 Dr.s in the research team. The direction now is undertaking several national and provincial scientific research projects such as the project from National Planning Office of Philosophy and Social -- Studies on the Talents in The Economic Development in Central and Western Regions, and the key soft science research project from Ministry of Railways-- Research on Railway Labor Management System Design.
