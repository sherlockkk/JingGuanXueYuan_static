Professional Introduction of Finance

Ⅰ. Goals for Professional Training
(1) Professional Profile: Basic Information of the Major Finance major was established in 1999 and enrolled about 75 students every year. Now there are more than 300students. Since 2007, financial major began to recruit both science and art students. Since 2013, finance major began to only recruit the first batch of undergraduates. There are 7 teachers in the finance teaching and research office, the majority of teachers have a master's degree or above. Their professional title structures are: senior 3, intermediate 4. At the same time, industry veteran from bank, insurance companies, securities companies and others were also hired as tutors off campus. The director of the financial major is mainly taken by the teachers of professional courses, which are more instructive for students' professional study. Professional Characteristics and Educational Achievements Students of finance major graduate with a bachelor's degree in economics, which is similar to economics, finance and international trade. Professional courses involve bank, security, and investment, insurance and so on. In order to facilitate the employment of students, finance major is divided into three aspects according to the changing economic form: bank, insurance and investment, which focus on the basic theory and knowledge of students. And the professional teaching plan has formed into a system, which is fully consistent with the school's running thought. Strengthen the practical segments, focus on the two internships, and students have a strong manual dexterity and got great graduates praise of employers. Some graduates have become the business backbone of the unit, and there are also some outstanding students who continue further study in key universities and this school. Employment Situation of Graduates There are nearly 800 graduates of 10 batches. Graduates are mainly employed by banks, insurance companies, fund companies, investment companies and futures companies, which are mainly located in Beijing, Shenzhen, Shanghai, Nanchang and Ganzhou, with high employment rate. As the entry of foreign financial institutions and increasing depth of institutional reform, the employment rate of the finance major will be better. (2) Professional Training Goals: This major cultivates professional personnel who possess theoretical knowledge and business skills of finance, and can work on related work in banks, security, investment, insurance and other economic management departments.

Ⅱ. Requirements of Professional Training
Students mainly study the basic theory and knowledge of finance, international finance, portfolio investment, insurance and other aspects and would get trained in related business, finally possessing primary ability to do practical work in the finance field. Graduates should obtain following knowledge, ability and quality: 1. Master basic theory and knowledge of finance; 2. Have fundamental ability to deal with business of banking, securities, investment, insurance and other aspects; 3. Familiar with the national policies, policies and regulations on finance; 4. Understand the theoretical preface and development trend of this discipline; 5. Master basic method for the literature retrieval, data query and a certain degree of scientific research and practical work ability.
